Key Manufacturing Regions

China's textile industry is concentrated in specific regions, each with unique specializations.

🌆 Shanghai

Specialty: High-end fashion, technical textiles

Home to premium garment factories and international trading companies. Best for quality-focused brands.

🏭 Guangzhou

Specialty: Fast fashion, wholesale

The world's largest garment wholesale market. Ideal for trendy, fast-turnaround production.

🧶 Keqiao (Shaoxing)

Specialty: Fabrics, textiles

Asia's largest textile market. Source any fabric type at competitive prices.
