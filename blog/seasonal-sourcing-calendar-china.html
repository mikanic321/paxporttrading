Seasonal Sourcing Calendar

Know when to buy. Save money. Avoid delays. Your complete guide to timing China purchases.

Strategy March 21, 2026 10 min read

Timing matters when sourcing from China. Order at the wrong time, and you could face 30-50% price increases, month-long delays, or quality issues. Order at the right time, and you'll save money and get faster delivery.

This calendar shows you exactly when to buy, when to avoid, and how to plan your sourcing around China's seasonal patterns.

Key Insight: Chinese New Year (January/February) causes the biggest disruption. Factories close for 2-4 weeks, and prices spike 20-30% before the holiday. Plan around it!

📅 Month-by-Month Sourcing Calendar

🎆 January - Chinese New Year Impact

Status: ⚠️ AVOID if possible

  • Factories close 2-4 weeks for CNY
  • Production stops completely
  • Shipping delays common
  • Prices 20-30% higher before holiday
Strategy: Place orders in December for January delivery. Avoid new orders until late February.

🧧 February - Post-CNY Recovery

Status: ⚠️ Challenging

  • Factories reopen mid-to-late February
  • Backlog of orders from before CNY
  • Longer lead times
  • Worker turnover (some don't return)
Strategy: Good time for planning and sampling. Wait for March for large orders.

🌸 March - Spring Season Begins

Status: ✅ GOOD

  • Factories fully operational
  • Stable pricing
  • Normal lead times
  • Good for spring/summer products
Strategy: Ideal time to place orders for summer delivery.

🌺 April - Peak Production

Status: ✅ EXCELLENT

  • Peak production capacity
  • Competitive pricing
  • Fast turnaround times
  • Best month for large orders
Strategy: Place major orders now for Q3 delivery.

🌞 May - Pre-Summer Rush

Status: ✅ GOOD

  • High production capacity
  • Stable pricing
  • Good for summer inventory
Strategy: Last chance for summer products. Start planning fall orders.

☀️ June - Summer Slowdown Begins

Status: ⚠️ Mixed

  • Some factories slower in heat
  • Dragon Boat Festival (1-3 days)
  • Good for fall/winter planning
Strategy: Good for sampling and product development. Avoid rush orders.

🏖️ July - Summer Lull

Status: ✅ GOOD for negotiation

  • Slower season = better prices
  • Factories eager for orders
  • Good time for custom development
Strategy: Negotiate better terms. Perfect for Q4 holiday products.

🌴 August - Pre-Peak Preparation

Status: ✅ EXCELLENT

  • Last month before peak season
  • Good pricing still available
  • Time for fall production
Strategy: Place holiday orders NOW. Prices rise in September.

🍂 September - Peak Season Begins

Status: ⚠️ BUSY

  • Mid-Autumn Festival (3 days)
  • National Day Golden Week prep
  • Prices start rising
  • Lead times extend
Warning: Last call for Christmas delivery. Order now or miss the boat!

🇨🇳 October - National Day Golden Week

Status: ⚠️ AVOID

  • Factories close Oct 1-7
  • Shipping delays
  • Pre-holiday price spikes
Strategy: Plan around Golden Week. Order before September 20.

🦃 November - Holiday Rush

Status: ⚠️ EXPENSIVE

  • Peak shipping season
  • Freight rates 30-50% higher
  • Port congestion
  • Only for urgent orders
Warning: Air freight only for Christmas. Sea freight too slow.

🎄 December - Year-End Rush

Status: ⚠️ MIXED

  • Factories rush to complete orders
  • Quality issues more common
  • Good for early 2026 planning
Strategy: Focus on 2026 planning. Avoid new production orders.

🎯 Best Times to Source

  • April-May: Best overall. Great prices, fast production.
  • July-August: Best for negotiation. Slow season = better deals.
  • March: Good for spring products, post-CNY recovery.

⚠️ Times to Avoid

  • January: Chinese New Year shutdown
  • February: Post-CNY chaos
  • October: Golden Week closure
  • November: Peak shipping rates

💰 Money-Saving Tips

  1. Order in April-May: Save 15-20% vs peak season
  2. Avoid pre-holiday rushes: Prices spike 20-30%
  3. Plan around CNY: Order 2 months before
  4. Use July lull: Negotiate better terms
  5. Book shipping early: Avoid peak rates
