Alibaba vs 1688

Which platform offers better prices and suppliers? A complete comparison guide.

Platform Comparison March 21, 2026 10 min read

When sourcing from China, you're likely to encounter two major platforms: Alibaba (the international giant) and 1688 (the domestic Chinese platform). Both connect buyers with Chinese suppliers, but they serve very different markets and offer distinct advantages.

In this guide, we'll break down the key differences, pricing, and help you decide which platform is right for your businessâ€”or if you should use both.

Quick Answer: Alibaba is better for international buyers who need English support and trade protection. 1688 offers 20-40% lower prices but requires Chinese language skills and doesn't offer buyer protection for international orders.

What is Alibaba?

Alibaba.com is the world's largest B2B marketplace, launched in 1999 by Jack Ma. It's designed specifically for international trade, connecting Chinese manufacturers and trading companies with buyers worldwide.

đźź  Alibaba Key Facts

  • Launched: 1999
  • Target market: International buyers
  • Language: English + 16 other languages
  • Suppliers: 200,000+ verified suppliers
  • Buyer protection: Trade Assurance program
  • Payment: Secure international payment options

What is 1688?

1688.com (pronounced "yi-liu-ba-ba") is Alibaba's domestic platform, launched in 2010. It's designed for Chinese domestic trade, connecting factories and wholesalers with Chinese businesses.

đź”µ 1688 Key Facts

  • Launched: 2010
  • Target market: Chinese domestic buyers
  • Language: Chinese only
  • Suppliers: 500,000+ factories and wholesalers
  • Buyer protection: Domestic Chinese protection only
  • Payment: Chinese payment methods (Alipay, WeChat Pay)

How to Buy from 1688 as a Foreigner

If you want to access 1688's lower prices, you have several options:

Option 1: Use a Sourcing Agent (Recommended)

A China-based sourcing agent can:

  • Search and communicate with 1688 suppliers
  • Negotiate prices on your behalf
  • Handle payment and logistics
  • Provide quality control
  • Consolidate orders from multiple suppliers

Cost: 5-10% commission, but you get the lower 1688 prices plus professional service.

Option 2: Use a 1688 Agent Service

Companies like:

  • 42Agent
  • Eng1688
  • Various Taobao/1688 buying agents

These services help foreigners buy from 1688 for a fee.

Option 3: Learn Chinese and DIY

If you're committed:

  • Use Google Translate or browser translation
  • Set up Alipay with a Chinese bank account
  • Use a Chinese freight forwarder
  • Accept the risks of no buyer protection
